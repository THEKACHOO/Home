TaskIt's Green Mission: Turning Everyday Tasks into Real Impact

How a mobile app transforms daily activities into tangible environmental impact through a new regulation for everyone to plant trees.

TaskIt introduces a simple yet powerful concept: complete tasks, plant trees. Every action on the platform contributes to global reforestation efforts.

TaskIt is a mobile application that connects users with taskers for various needs. What sets it apart is its commitment to environmental sustainability through a clear and actionable tree-planting regulation.

The platform believes that technology should serve both people and the planet. By integrating environmental action into its core functionality, TaskIt makes sustainability accessible to everyone.

Every Completed Task = One Tree Planted

Setiap tugas yang diselesaikan seperti paket yang dikirimkan oleh kurir, dan setiap paket yang tuntas berarti satu pohon baru ditanam.

Every completed task is like a package delivered by a courier, and every completed package means one new tree planted.

New Regulation: Everyone Can Plant Trees

TaskIt has implemented a clear, three-tier system that ensures every achievement on the platform contributes to reforestation. This regulation makes environmental action accessible to all users.

VIP User Achievement

Every time a VIP user completes their loyalty card, TaskIt plants one tree. This rewards dedicated users while contributing to environmental restoration.

Current: 6 trees planted by VIP users

Platform Milestones

For every 100 tasks completed on the TaskIt platform, one tree is planted. This ensures the entire community's efforts contribute to environmental impact.

Current: 9 trees from 900+ tasks

Tasker Champions

Every Tasker Champion who reaches 100 task points receives a tree planted in their honor. This rewards top performers while supporting reforestation.

Current: 5 trees for Tasker Champions

How It Works

TaskIt operates on a simple principle: connect users who need tasks done with taskers who can complete them. The platform handles everything from simple errands to specialized services.

The green initiative integrates seamlessly into this system. As users complete tasks and achieve milestones, trees are automatically planted through verified reforestation partners.

Transparency is key. Every tree planted is tracked and visible to users, creating a direct connection between daily activities and environmental impact.
