Kafka Company

Family Heritage • London • New York • Jakarta

Founded by the Kafka family in London, our company has maintained a tradition of excellence through generations. In 2026, we expanded to Southeast Asia with our new branch in South Jakarta, introducing our innovative KACHOO brand to the region. Our monochromatic philosophy reflects our commitment to clarity, precision, and timeless elegance.
